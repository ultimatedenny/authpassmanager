<p>Easily and securely keep track of all your Passwords!</p>

<p>AuthPass is a stand alone password manager with support for the popular and proven Keepass (kdbx) format. 
Store your passwords, share across all your devices and easily find them whenever you need to login.</p>

<ul>
<li>All your passwords in one place.<li>
<li>Generate secure random passwords for each of your accounts.</li>
<li>Quick Unlock secured with biometric lock (android only right now)</li>
<li>Keep track of your accounts across the web.</li>
<li>App available for Mac, iOS, Android, Linux and Windows.</li>
<li>Open Source available on https://github.com/authpass/authpass/</li>
<li>Contributions welcome and encouraged ;-) (Always looking for developers, translators, documentation writers, UI designer, etcc. :) ), just join our discord channel.</li>
</ul>
